WPJobAds 1.2.3
==============

Welcome and thank you for purchasing WPJobAds, a WordPres job board plugin that lets you sell job ads and keep 100% of the profits to yourself.

Minimum Requirements:
1. WordPress 2.5 or newer
2. PayPal Premier or Business account (optional)

- EULA.TXT (The End User License Agreement)
- README.TXT (This file your are now reading)
- wpjobads.php (The main plugin file)

- wpjobads.po
- manual (Manual in HTML format)
